【How to Play】

Funny sentences are falling down the screen! Player 1 and Player 2 take turns protecting their scores.

  • The falling boxes will change color to match the active player's turn! 🔵/💗

  • If a sentence has BAD GRAMMAR (e.g., "Does he goes...?") 👉 DO NOT CLICK IT! Let it fall.
  • Clicking a bad sentence or letting a good sentence slip away will lose you points!

Battle Over!

💡 Quick Grammar Reminders:
1. After Does, always use the base action verb: "Does your father go..." (NOT goes).
2. Short answers keep it simple: "Yes, he usually does." or "No, he never does."
